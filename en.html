FAQ
What is a DAO?

DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) is a decentralized autonomous organization, a legal entity in the digital world. Decentralization means that people govern themselves. Yes, they can appoint a director, but in fact, they govern themselves through the Protocol. The Protocol is a smart contract, a blockchain, and all the automation that goes on. The blockchain provides reliable data protection because it is impossible to change information retroactively. Information is automatically duplicated on different servers. If data is deleted from one server, it is immediately restored to another. That is why countries that are not afraid of corruption have started using blockchain to maintain registries, register marriages, etc. This increases transparency and reduces the possibility of corruption.

What is the legal framework of DAO Asgard?

The legal framework of DAO Asgard:

LAW of the Republic of Panama No. 25 of June 12, 1995 "On Private Foundations"

Package of laws adopted in the Republic of Panama in 2021-2022: No. 697, dedicated to the regulation of blockchain, smart contracts, and crypto.

These laws have united the world of blockchain technologies with the laws on company incorporation that have existed for more than 25 years.

How do I officially become a co-owner of real estate?

A member of such a DAO receives legal protection of his or her rights, namely:

  • DAO is a registered legal entity. A DAO as a legal entity can enter into agreements with any other legal entities and individuals (unlike an unregistered DAO);
  • The powers of the governing bodies are clearly regulated, which minimizes the risks that funds may be used for purposes other than those of the DAO (targeted use of funds);
  • Blockchain and smart contracts ensure the transparency of DAO activities;
  • each DAO participant can influence the decisions made by the DAO (for example, which lots will be put up for auction);
  • legal work with tokens and crypto;
  • Conclusion of transactions in a transparent and clear manner
How do you distribute the money from the sale?

General structure of transactions:

DAO member ------- DAO ------- Counterparties

To simplify, the DAO represents the interests of DAO participants in transactions. At the same time, the contract does not need to include all lot participants. Lot participants are recorded using blockchain technologies (information about them cannot be deleted or changed).

Each DAO participant will have his or her own token, which can be considered as a "party ticket" or "fitness club card". This means that you have the status of a DAO member. DAO members (as stated in the Charter) are the ultimate beneficiaries of the company's activities. The final beneficiaries are the beneficiaries, the persons who benefit from the work of the DAO. The DAO structure fully meets the needs of Asgard's clients and partners.

A DAO buys, for example, 5,000 square meters of real estate at the expense of its members (a contract is concluded between the DAO and the owner of the real estate) and becomes the legal owner of these 5,000 square meters. In other words, all members actually own the DAO, and the DAO owns the real estate. To account for the rights of DAO participants to this real estate, tokens are issued and you receive them in proportion to your share in the property. A separate token is issued for each newly acquired property in an amount that is always proportional to the square meters of the property. Thus, each DAO participant is assigned his or her share in a particular property
